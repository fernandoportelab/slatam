About
Destilería Carúpano nestles in a valley amid the humid and fertile earth of the Hacienda Altamira in Macarapana. Founded in 1762, by a Spanish captain Felix del Fierro, the Hacienda Altamira is a perfect paragon for the 250 years old history of Carupano s Rum.
