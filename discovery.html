About Discovery services

1. Customer Research Service

Slatam Discovery presents your business to potential customers around the world who are ready to buy or hire. When you join Slatam, you quickly connect to a global network of companies and buyers. You can quickly and efficiently scale existing relationships with customers, as well as discover and gain new connections with this capture solution.

2. Business ready to sell on the internet

Launching an online business can be exciting and profitable. It is a great way to complement an existing income stream or even to become one's sole occupation. Many people and companies have had tremendous success, some of them literally earning millions of dollars a year, even after starting in the zero zone, without knowledge of the Internet beyond the basics.

3. Create an LLC in the United States and have a quick and easy legal bank account

Slatam Mundi is a safe, and easy-to-use platform for forming a company. By removing lengthy paperwork, bank visits, legal complexity, numerous fees, and non-obvious decisions about what services to use, Slatam Mundi helps you launch your startup from anywhere in the world.
